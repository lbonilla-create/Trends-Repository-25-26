My Ultimate College Life Web Page

By Lance Simon Angelo B. Bonilla

Introduction

My name is Lance and I am a 2nd year college student in Ateneo de Naga University. I am 20 years of age and I live in the Philippines, Naga City. I try to balance my personal, work, and academic life as best as I can, and I am always trying to learn improve myself as a person and my lifestyle. Personally, I really do not enjoy going to school and talking to people.

College Must-Haves

  • High-End PC: For doing heavy tasks, multitasking, learning, gaming, and doing schoolwork.
  • Apple Airpods: For on the go listening to music, comes with the best noise cancelling and transparency mode.
  • Nintendo 3DS or PS Vita: Portable handhelds for short entertainment during gaps.
  • iPod 6th Gen Classic: When I want to listen to music for longer periods of time or full albums in the library.
  • Trade paperback comic book: Comics in trade format I can bring anywhere, and catching up on my TBR's.
  • iPad Air M3: Doing school work outside the house, browsing, and entertainment.
  • Water bottle: For staying hydrated.
  • Fire outfit: So people know I have the fit on and I have taste.

Daily Routine

  1. Morning: Getting ready for school.
  2. Afternoon: Being held hostage at school.
  3. Evening: Dedicated time for school work and/or band practice.
  4. Midnight: Unwinding with a film, TV series, an album on record vinyl, or catching up on comic TBR's.

  • Playing guitar, bass, drums, and practicing my scream vocals
  • Practicing and performing with my band (Dwell)
  • Collecting and reading comics (pull list from LCS in Ortigas)
  • Reading classic literature, philosophy, and horror fiction
  • Short sessions of playing video games
  • Golfing
  • Bowling
  • Ice skating
  • Airsoft (when with friends only)
  • Collecting physical media and curating my collection (DVD's, Blu-Ray's, CD's, vinyl records, books, comic books, video games)
